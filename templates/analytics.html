What Do These Risk Levels Mean?
🟢 Clean
No threats found. The system looks healthy. No action needed — keep running regular scans to stay protected.
🟡 Suspicious
Some unusual activity was detected that could be harmless or could be a threat. A security expert should review it before taking action.
🔴 Infected
A rootkit or serious threat was confirmed. The system has been compromised. Immediate remediation is strongly recommended.
✅ Remediated
Threats were found and then removed automatically. The system has been cleaned. A follow-up scan is recommended to confirm.
🔍 What is a Rootkit?
A rootkit is a hidden piece of software that lets attackers secretly control your server. RootSentry hunts for these by looking for things hidden from normal tools.
📊 Anomaly Score
A number that adds up the severity of all findings. Think of it like a temperature — the higher it is, the sicker the system. Under 10 is healthy.
