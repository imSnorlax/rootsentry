Risk Level Reference
🟢 Clean
No threats found. System looks healthy. Keep running regular scans to stay protected.
🟡 Suspicious
Unusual activity detected that may or may not be a threat. A security expert should review before acting.
🔴 Infected
Rootkit or serious threat confirmed. System is compromised — immediate remediation required.
✅ Remediated
Threats found and removed automatically. A follow-up scan is recommended to confirm cleanliness.
🔍 What is a Rootkit?
Hidden software that lets attackers secretly control your server. RootSentry hunts for these by finding things deliberately hidden from normal tools.
📊 Anomaly Score
A severity-weighted score across all findings. Think of it like a fever — the higher it is, the sicker the system. Under 10 is healthy.
